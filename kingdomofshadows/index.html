About the Game

Kingdom of Shadows was developed by two students as the final project of the Informatics Engineering course at Polytechnic Institute of Bragança. It's built on Unreal Engine 4 with free assets from the community and takes advantage of the powerful Blueprints system.

Inspired by games like Trine 2, INSIDE and Dark Souls, Kingdom of Shadows is a sidescroller where there are various obstacles, traps and enemies that make the game challenging, yet rewarding. The player has access to two playable characters and can switch between them on the fly, providing alternatives on how to defeat enemies and progress through the levels.
